28 Years of Experience

Sunil Polam Reddy is a seasoned professional with 28 years of experience as a working professional and business entrepreneur. His journey into wellness coaching stems from his own transformative experiences with Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP) and emotional intelligence practices.

As a certified NLP Advanced Practitioner and member of the International Coaching Federation, Sunil combines practical business acumen with proven wellness techniques to help clients overcome limitations, manage stress, and achieve their personal and professional goals.

His approach is personalized, empathetic, and results-oriented, focusing on empowering individuals to unlock their full potential and live more fulfilling lives.
